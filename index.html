Welcome to VJTI E-Library by Preliminary Examinations 2025

While students are expected to procure some of their course materials independently, VJTI offers a wide array of specialized resources to complement their studies and enrich their academic journey.

The VJTI Library stands as a pivotal resource hub, providing essential information support throughout your academic pursuits. Our knowledgeable library staff are available to assist you from your initial days on campus to the completion of your project, dissertation work, and preparation for your future career.

Since, this website is still in the developing stage, our technical team has confined ourselves to develop this e-library facility for the 2026 Higher Secondary School Certificate Examination. In the upcoming months, we'll extend this facility to all the branches of VJTI.
