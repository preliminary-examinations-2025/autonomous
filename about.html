About the VJTI e-Library

The VJTI e-Library is a digital initiative designed to empower students, faculty, and researchers with seamless access to academic resources anytime, anywhere. As one of Mumbai's most prestigious institutes, VJTI has always aimed to blend traditional values with modern technological advancements. Our e-library platform is a testament to that vision, offering e-books, previous year question papers, syllabi, lab manuals, and reference materials all under one roof. With an intuitive interface and regularly updated content, the e-library ensures that users have access to the most relevant and up-to-date information. Whether you're preparing for exams, conducting research, or just exploring new domains, the VJTI e-Library is your academic companion—efficient, accessible, and always evolving.

History

The concept of a digital e-library at VJTI took shape in 2024 during the post-pandemic academic transition. With increased reliance on virtual education, a need was felt for a centralized knowledge hub that could transcend physical barriers. Spearheaded by dedicated faculty and tech-savvy students, the e-library project began as a campus-wide collaboration. It has since grown into a full-fledged academic repository, becoming an indispensable resource for thousands of users across departments.
